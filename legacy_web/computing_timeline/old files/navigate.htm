How to Navigate the Computing Timeline


  • Click on the M icons for more information about a computing event.
  • Link directly to online sources used in within the "bubble" text for events.
  • Place your cursor in the timeline itself and scroll left or right to view the History of Computing events through the years!

The Timeline was researched and compiled by Alice Goff, U-M School of Information graduate student assistant and Nancy Deromedi, associate archivist, Summer 2007. The authors of the site invite commentary. Please contact Associate Archivist, Nancy Deromedi, 734.764.3482 or deromedi@umich.edu.
